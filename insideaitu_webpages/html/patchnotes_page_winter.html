#INSIDE AITU WINTER UPDATE,
NEW YEAR PRESENTS, NEW STUDENTS, SPECIAL OUTFITS, AND MORE!!

27.12.2021

By Inside AITU team.

It’s New Year’s Eve, which means it’s time to give gifts. In New Year’s Eve, students will give players 7 gifts in one week before New Year. There’s also one secret gift, but you have to turn it on and find it! Go to the main tree in the lobby every day for seven days! Under the tree will be various costumes, gadgets, boot screens, smiley face, spray and equipment. If you miss any opening days, just come back at any time before the end of Event (January 6 at 9:00 Nur-sultan time.) to open them.

While you’re enjoying the holiday decorations around university, you can notice Ayaz Ata (people from the Dean’s Office). Winter patrols. He’ll be throwing birthday presents! But if you find yourself on his bad side (he’s a naughty list), he won’t hesitate to take you to the retake...

New Year’s Eve is no reason not to play, come in with your friends and enjoy the snow and normal weather, not this cold outside.

With snow comes new mechanics:

  • Throw away others and throw slippery ice mice on their feet with cooling grenades. (Also interesting to apply to yourself.)
  • Use a hidden snowman to disguise yourself as a snowman and hide.
  • Throw snowballs.
  • Throw away the holiday presents! to open them and take their loot.
