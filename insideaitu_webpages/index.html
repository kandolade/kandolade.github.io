#INSIDE <AITU> - a
game-project from the students of Astana IT University to explore
the university and
the study of programming
#INSIDE <AITU> is to help future students of Astana IT University to learn the field of programming. The game provides you with a complete uni building for future orientation, various tasks from the contest, as well as many interesting conversations with the students of the uni. The game is a project for the university and release dates are unknown.
